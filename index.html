About Me

Hello You,

My name is Heavenly, and I'm from Ponce, Puerto Rico, a beautiful island full of undiscovered talent. I enjoy reading, writing, and playing video games. I mostly read manga and write poetry, but honestly, I write whatever comes to mind. I don't follow a specific genre or style; I just let the ideas take me where they want to go.

When I was 15, I finally started letting people read my work and they loved it. That's when I realized I didn't have to keep my writing to myself forever. I could share it with the world.

One memory that always stays with me happened when I was 16. I wrote a poem for a class assignment that I unfortunately lost, and I still have no idea where it went. I turned in the only copy to my teacher and never got it back. What mattered, though, was his reaction, he cried while reading it, and I loved it. Seeing someone feel something real because of my words was the best part of the entire experience.

That's why I write. I love connecting with people and seeing how my work moves them.

On this website, you'll be able to read and purchase my writing, explore my thoughts and memories, and discover stories I once never imagined sharing.

Thank you so much for believing in me.
