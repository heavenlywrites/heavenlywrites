How to use this bot
🧚 Fairy Counter (Channel Points)

When a viewer redeems your Catch a Fairy channel point reward, the bot automatically responds in chat with their name and their total fairy count. The count saves permanently — it never resets unless you hit the Reset button.


Make sure the reward name in the box matches your reward name exactly on Twitch. It's case-insensitive so capitalisation doesn't matter.

💬 Chat Trigger Words

Add a trigger word or phrase (e.g. !discord) and a response (e.g. Join us at discord.gg/link). Any time someone types that word in chat, the bot replies automatically.


Use the ON/OFF toggle to disable a trigger without deleting it. The cooldown setting stops the same trigger from spamming if multiple people say it at once.

⚠ Keep this browser tab open while streaming — the bot disconnects if the tab is closed. Triggers and fairy count are saved in your browser automatically.
