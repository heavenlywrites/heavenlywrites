How to use
🧚 Fairy Counter

When someone redeems your Catch a Fairy channel points reward, the bot replies in chat with their name and total fairy count. The count saves permanently — only resets if you click the reset button.


Make sure the reward name field matches your reward name exactly on Twitch (case doesn't matter).

💬 Trigger Words

Add a trigger word (e.g. !discord) and response. When anyone types that word in chat, the bot replies instantly. Use the ON/OFF toggle to pause a trigger without deleting it.


The cooldown prevents the same trigger spamming if many people type it at once.
